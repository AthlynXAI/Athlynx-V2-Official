## Health-Tech Commercialization Guardrails

Athlete BioSignal OS must remain disciplined as it moves from concept to product. The platform should be described as **read-only informational monitoring**, **sensor lifecycle tracking**, **data freshness awareness**, **athlete context**, and **clinician/guardian/team-safe summaries**. Public and partner-facing language must not claim diagnosis, medical treatment, emergency response replacement, insulin dosing, or pump control unless a regulated pathway, clinical governance, and counsel-reviewed claims are in place.

| Area | Guardrail |
|---|---|
| CGM / glucose | Monitor and normalize readings; do not prescribe insulin or control pumps. |
| Heart / ECG | Summarize user-authorized data; do not diagnose arrhythmias without cleared clinical workflow. |
| Oxygen / respiration | Show trends and freshness; do not claim emergency detection or diagnosis. |
| Brain / concussion | Track impact/cognitive context; do not diagnose concussion or clear return-to-play. |
| Blood flow / BP | Use trend language unless using validated, cleared clinical devices under proper governance. |
| AI reasoning | AI may summarize and flag context; AI must not replace clinicians, emergency care, or source devices. |
