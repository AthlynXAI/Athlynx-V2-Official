## Winning Message

AthlynXAI GlucoAthlete OS is an AI-native Digital Health layer for diabetic athletes. It connects CGM data such as Libre 3 Plus, athlete training context, nutrition, sleep, symptoms, emergency contacts, and clinician-approved care plans into one consent-controlled, audit-ready athlete profile. The first release focuses on monitoring, trend intelligence, fueling support, emergency escalation, and provider-ready summaries. Direct insulin dosing and pump control are treated as regulated future capabilities, not unapproved MVP features.

## 60-Second Pitch

AthlynXAI is building GlucoAthlete OS, an AI-native Digital Health operating system for diabetic athletes. Families today stitch together CGM apps, phone alerts, coaches, parents, school staff, nutrition notes, and medical instructions without one trusted athlete operating layer. GlucoAthlete OS connects CGM signals, sport context, nutrition, sleep, symptoms, emergency contacts, and clinician-approved care plans into one consent-controlled and audit-ready profile. The platform does not replace doctors or prescribe insulin in its unregulated version. It monitors trends, flags risk, guides fueling education, escalates emergencies, and prepares provider-ready summaries. With Nebius, AthlynXAI can scale real-time prediction, alert prioritization, personalization, secure inference, and athlete health intelligence.
