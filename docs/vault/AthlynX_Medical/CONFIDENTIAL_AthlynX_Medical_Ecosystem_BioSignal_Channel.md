## Universal BioSignal Channel

The Universal BioSignal Channel is the connector that makes the platform scalable. Every device or partner becomes an adapter. Every adapter produces the same normalized data structure: consent, source, athlete, signal domain, metric, value, unit, trend, timestamp, freshness, device lifecycle, alert state, escalation contact, privacy status, and audit proof.

| Channel Stage | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Consent Gateway | Athlete, guardian, clinician, or caregiver permission. |
| Device Adapter | Libre, Dexcom, Apple Health, Garmin, WHOOP, Oura, hydration, heat, cardiac, oxygen, brain, blood-flow, biomedical, robotics. |
| Identity Redaction | No full serials, codes, or PHI exposed on public surfaces. |
| Signal Normalizer | Converts all device data into one schema. |
| Sensor Lifecycle Engine | Tracks Libre 14-day sensors, Dexcom sessions, patches, pods, and future devices. |
| Alert Rules Engine | Non-diagnostic alerts and trend flags. |
| Human Escalation Router | Athlete, parent, coach, trainer, clinician, or emergency plan contacts. |
| Vault Audit Log | Evidence, consent, source, time, action, and proof chain. |
| Partner API/MCP Layer | Read-only API/MCP tools and future approved connectors. |

## Safety Boundary

AthlynX Medical and BioSignal OS are not currently marketed as medical devices. They do not diagnose, cure, treat, prescribe insulin, control pumps, clear athletes for play, replace the Libre app, replace clinicians, or replace emergency services. Any feature that crosses into medical interpretation, treatment guidance, device control, return-to-play clearance, or emergency action must move through clinical validation, legal review, FDA/SaMD pathway review, cybersecurity review, and partner approval.
