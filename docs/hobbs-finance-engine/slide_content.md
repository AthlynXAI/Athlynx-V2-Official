## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM ON THE FINANCE DESK TODAY
**Headline:** Every Deal Costs You 45–90 Minutes You Don't Have

**Key points:**
- Finance managers re-key the same customer data into 4–6 lender portals by hand on every single deal
- Deal jackets — buyer's order, TILA disclosures, retail installment contract, GAP and service-contract forms — are assembled manually, one document at a time
- OFAC checks, Red Flags identity verification, and compliance disclosures are done separately, creating audit gaps
- Customers sit in the box and cool off while paperwork is being built — killing CSI scores and F&I product attachment rates
- Kicked contracts from stip failures cost the dealership real money and delay funding by days

**Bottom stat bar:** Industry average F&I deal time: 73 minutes | Kicked contract rate: 18% | CSI impact from long box time: -22 pts

**Visual direction:** Dark slide, amber/red accent colors for the pain points. Bold numbers in electric blue. Clean two-column layout — problem list left, stat callouts right.
