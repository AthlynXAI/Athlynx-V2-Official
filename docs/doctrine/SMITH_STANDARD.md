## Why this doctrine exists

AthlynXAI is a 180+ page, 100,000+ line operating system inside a category where Opendorse, Hudl, PlayerProfiler, and 247Sports have already raised the visual and conversion bar. They publish clean mobile pages, athlete-action creative, short conversion copy, brand proof, lead forms, reports, and paid social funnels.

**Meeting that bar is not enough.** We have to meet it, then go further by connecting every post, profile, video, form, email, SMS, calendar event, and lead into the CRM and the Full Stack Layer Cake Tokenization layer.

The Smith Standard is the name for that discipline. It is the answer to the *12-to-90,000+ athlete problem*: while competitors claim athlete networks at scale, AthlynXAI must treat acquisition as a product system, not a slogan.

## The one-line standard

**Every product surface, athlete profile, social post, campaign page, onboarding flow, and CRM action must be forged, sharpened, tested, and made useful before it reaches a user.**

No generic posts. No amateur graphics. No motivational filler. No stock content. No disconnected social updates. No claims without proof.

## The Maddux Sequence

Greg Maddux did not throw the hardest fastball. He won 355 games by **commanding** location, sequence, and pace — beating hitters who had louder tools. AthlynXAI applies the same operating discipline to every external action: product launches, athlete onboarding, investor outreach, content publishing, and growth campaigns.

The Maddux Sequence has four pitches. Every shipped artifact, every outbound message, and every capital conversation moves through them in order. No skipping pitches.

### Pitch 1 — Setup (NDA-first / proof-first / earn-the-room)
- Lead with the smallest reasonable artifact that proves the work is real.
- For investors: NDA before deck, deck before cap table, cap table before SAFE.
- For athletes: claim profile before upload, upload before invite, invite before campaign.
- For social: proof asset before claim, claim before CTA, CTA before paid amplification.

### Pitch 2 — Spot (precise placement, no spray)
- One message per asset. One action per page. One ask per email.
- Targeted distribution — the right twelve people beat the wrong twelve thousand.
- Mobile-first layout, athlete-led imagery, two-minute scan rule for coaches and parents.

### Pitch 3 — Change of pace (depth on demand)
- Have the deep version ready: full deck, full reel, full CRM record, full proof bundle.
- Surface depth only when the prospect leans in. Never lead with the long version.
- The 45-minute meeting, the founder testimony, the cap table walkthrough — these are pitch 3.

### Pitch 4 — Put-away (the close, on approval only)
- Anchor on the desired tier (e.g., investor at $200K of $50K–$250K range; athlete at full-profile claim, not just email capture).
- Do not negotiate the foundation (cap, doctrine, brand voice).
- All put-away actions are human-approved. Agents propose; Chad disposes.

### Maddux × Smith Standard mapping

| Smith Standard pillar | Maddux pitch that carries it |
|---|---|
| Forged creative | Pitch 1 — Setup |
| Proof before claims | Pitch 1 — Setup |
| Mobile-first flow | Pitch 2 — Spot |
| No generic posts | Pitch 2 — Spot |
| CRM-connected action | Pitch 3 — Change of pace |
| Competitive discipline | Pitch 3 — Change of pace |
| Approval control | Pitch 4 — Put-away |

## Forward-Only Deployment Gate

The Smith Standard includes a deployment rule: **code is not complete until it is verified on the correct deployment target.** Preview work and production work must be labeled separately at every step.

| Gate | Required check |
|---|---|
| Repo gate | Confirm repository, branch, PR number, and commit SHA. |
| Identity gate | Confirm commit email is `chaddozier75@gmail.com` and prohibited identities are absent. |
| Build gate | Run local TypeScript/build checks when dependencies allow. |
| Secret gate | Scan changed files for tokens, passwords, API keys, `.env`, and credential material. |
| Vercel gate | Confirm deployment target is the correct `athlynx-platform` project. |
| Route gate | Confirm the new route returns HTTP 200 on the preview deployment. |
| Production gate | Confirm whether the route is preview-only or live production; do not blur the two. |
| Sentry gate | Run read-only unresolved issue check after deploy. |

| Backup gate | Confirm source snapshot or GitHub commit history is recoverable. |
| Approval gate | Chad approves merges, production promotions, DB writes, and external communications. |

## The 12-to-90,000+ growth engine

The first measurable Smith Standard target: move from ~12 active/internal/test/partner users to 90,000+ verified athletes without compromising any pillar.

The required loops:

1. **Claim profile** — career-stage badge, proof bundle scaffold, E2EE on by default.
2. **Upload proof** — highlights, stats, training evidence, NIL deliverables.
3. **Invite teammate / invite coach** — mentorship lineage activates.
4. **Connect social accounts** — Instagram, X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Vimeo, Hudl-style video surfaces feed `social_identity_link` into CRM.
5. **Book evaluation** — calendar-integrated, two-minute coach scan rule honored.
6. **Join campaign** — Athlete Playbook funnel or sport-specific lead engine.
7. **Share verified profile** — public-facing share card honors E2EE visibility settings.

Each step is a Maddux Pitch 1 / Pitch 2 setup. The platform never skips to Pitch 4 (the close) without earning Pitch 3 (proof depth).
