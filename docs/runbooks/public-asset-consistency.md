# Public Asset Consistency Runbook

AthlynXAI public deployments must not expose private financial templates, investor archives, due diligence files, raw vault backups, or unfinalized partner artifacts. Public pages may describe controlled capabilities, but partner names, logos, terms, and hardware/product claims stay gated until Chad confirms the deal is finalized and public-safe.

## Current operating rule

1. Keep private/investor/due-diligence files in the private Google Drive vault, not in `public/` or `client/public/`.
2. Do not show unapproved partner logos or claim active partnerships from public pages.
3. Use generic, request-access language for research or partner-dependent offerings until commercial approval is recorded.
4. Treat screenshots, vault files, and inbox evidence as proof materials, not public assets.
