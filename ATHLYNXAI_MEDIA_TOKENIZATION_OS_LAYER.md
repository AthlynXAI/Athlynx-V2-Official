# AthlynXAI Media Tokenization OS Layer

**Status:** Production doctrine baked into AthlynXAI OS.  
**Scope:** Podcast, social media, AXN streaming service, approved brand guardrails, team operating model, and tokenization layer.

## Operating Doctrine

AthlynXAI OS is the source of truth. Spotify for Creators, Spotify, Suno, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, Vercel, and other services are external rails. The platform owns the identity, content rules, athlete data, credit logic, and publishing decisions. External rails distribute, host, or generate supporting assets only after proof and approval.

## Approved Brand Law

The visible brand universe is restricted to **AXN**, **XAI**, **AthlynX**, and **AthlynXAI**. Media assets, pages, posts, podcast materials, streaming screens, apparel overlays, and athlete equipment branding must use those names and marks only. Podcast and studio scenes should remain darker and athlete-first, with podcasters serving the story rather than becoming the focus.

## Execution Rule

Research outputs are not shipped until they are reviewed, committed, deployed, and verified. Connector availability is not assumed. Every external write, push, deployment, public post, payment, database mutation, or account action requires proof of the exact rail and an intentional execution step.
