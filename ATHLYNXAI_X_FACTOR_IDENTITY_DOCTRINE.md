# AthlynXAI Permanent X-Factor Identity Doctrine

**Owner:** Chad A. Dozier  
**Status:** Permanent operating rule  
**Scope:** AthlynXAI OS, X-Factor product surfaces, logo/icon usage, social cards, public website, app launcher/icon direction, profile pages, sport pages, investor materials, and internal doctrine.

## Core rule

AthlynXAI is **the X of the industry**. The X-Factor identity is the primary symbolic mark for the AthlynXAI competitive position: the platform that crosses sport, recruiting, NIL, content, calendar, messaging, compliance, and monetization into one operating system.

The approved X-Factor direction is a **stealth black foundation with a clean white or ice-white X mark**. It should look premium, sharp, serious, and unmistakable. The symbol must be used as an original AthlynXAI identity mark, not as copied third-party branding or placeholder art.

## Permanent identity rules

| Area | Permanent rule |
|---|---|
| Positioning | AthlynXAI is the X of the industry: the cross-point where athlete identity, performance, NIL, recruiting, media, and business operations meet. |
| Icon direction | Black or metallic-black field with a clean white/ice-white X-Factor mark. Controlled cyan/ice-blue accents are allowed only when they support the stealth theme. |
| Logo usage | The X-Factor mark belongs on X-Factor surfaces, AthlynXAI hero moments, app icons, social cards, and premium product identity moments. |
| Visual constraints | No gold, no yellow, no rainbow colors, no unnecessary emojis, no copied screenshots, no placeholder marks, and no unapproved third-party marks. |
| Quality bar | The mark must look native to the platform and premium enough to represent the full AthlynXAI OS. |
| Exceptions | Any exception requires explicit owner approval for that specific asset or page. |

## Relationship to the broader ecosystem

The X-Factor mark represents AthlynXAI’s product identity and industry position. It does not erase the Dozier Holdings Group ecosystem hierarchy. DHG ecosystem marks and subsidiary-specific logos must remain separate where legally or operationally required, while the X-Factor mark is the primary AthlynXAI product/OS identity signal.

## Implementation requirement

Every future AthlynXAI design, app icon, social post, sport page, profile surface, public page, and deck must check this doctrine before release. If a surface claims to represent AthlynXAI’s X-Factor or app identity, it must use the stealth-mode X-Factor direction and comply with `ATHLYNXAI_STEALTH_VISUAL_DOCTRINE.md`.
